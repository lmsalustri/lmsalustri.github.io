Partnerships

Heebner Career Development Center

The HCDC provides career resources for neurodivergent students, including the Night with Industry, which takes place the night before the semiannual career fair. Students can network with recruiters in a more relaxed setting. No formal dress code is enforced.

Student Development & Counseling Center

We have partnered with the SDCC to launch the Navigating Neurodiversity support group. It currently meets on Mondays at 2 PM in Daniels 139 for the duration of C Term 2025.
